About Us

The Devard Bed and Breakfast is renowned for its warm and friendly welcome. With its high standards of quality and care, the visitor here is rewarded with that unique hospitality which is so much a feature of the West of Ireland. Devard is ideally located close to Castlebar on the main Westport Road. Email us now with your booking or submit a reservation request online and we will get back to you within 24 hours.

About Castlebar

Castlebar is an ideal touring location. Achill Island, Ceide Fields, Ballintubber Abbey, Foxford Wollen Mills are some of the places you may wish to visit while staying at Devard.The town also hosts Ireland's largest indoor entertainment complex which includes a 10 pin bowling alley and 7 screen cinema. County Mayo boasts thirteen blue flag beaches, many within a short driving distance of Castlebar.
