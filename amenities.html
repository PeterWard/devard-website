Devard bed and breakfast is located in the heart of the West. It is close to all the amenities available in the town of Castlebar and is situated ideally in respect to the other tourist destinations throughout Mayo. Take a look at some of the attractions listed below and check out the map that displays these spots in relation to our bed and breakfast.

Museum of Country Life

Travel through time and experience Irish history. The National Museum of Ireland – Country Life is set in modern exhibition galleries (above left) in the spectacular grounds of Turlough Park House (above right) and surrounded by magnificent gardens and lake.

View Site

Lough Lannagh

Take a stroll around the beautiful Lough Lannagh. Here you'll find impressive views of Croagh Patrick, bustling wildlife and the clamness of the magnificent lake itself. The walk is looped so you can drop your car off and circle back. Check out the mini documentary on the swans of this lake on the right.

View Site

Mayo Peace Park

After a refreshing walk around Lough Lannagh why not visit the peace park which pays homage to the Irish soldiers that lost their lives in World War 1. Their names are enscribed on an impressive piece of marble curvature.

View Site

Croagh Patrick

Experience breathtaking views of Clew bay and all around from the summit of Croagh Patrick. Every year thousands from all around Ireland and beyond climb to the peak of this famous mountain for Reek Sunday. Many climb this famous natural landmark throughout the year to catch a glimpse of Mayo from a magnificent viewpoint.
